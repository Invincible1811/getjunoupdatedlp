Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Powerful tools designed around how midwives actually work.

Your Optimized Calendar

Managing home visits, clinic hours, and birth windows is a logistical puzzle. Juno solves it automatically — giving you more time for what matters.

Seamless Communication

Centralise patient interactions into one professional hub. Stay connected without the burnout.

Digital Diary

Every pregnancy is a story. Get a 360-degree view of the mother's health and progress.

Route Planning

Juno plans appointments by location to minimise your travel time.

Practice at a Glance

Track visits, capacity, revenue, and billing in real-time — replacing spreadsheets with clarity.
